## Notes on what to change before sending

- **Subject line:** drop the word "AI" if you think the doctor's tech-averse — replace with "patient follow-up assistant"
- **"$99/mo"** — only mention this in Version B. Version A skips price entirely; you'll discuss it after the demo.
- **The hardware photo** — attach a photo of the printed Adapt case if you have one. Makes it feel real.
- **The website link** — only include if adapixai.com is live and the reservation form works
- **Do not attach a deck.** Get them to a 10-minute demo. Decks slow this down.

## After they say yes

1. Print the case and assemble the Pi
2. Visit them with the device + a laptop
3. OAuth-connect their Gmail or 365 (the flow you just built)
4. Walk through the dashboard with the front desk
5. Leave it plugged in. Check in at day 3, day 7, day 30.
