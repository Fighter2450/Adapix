## The problem (in your language)

A general dentist refers a patient to you for wisdom teeth, an implant, or a more complex case. **30-40% of those referrals never become completed procedures.** Some don't call to schedule. Some come in for the consult and walk out saying "let me think about it." Some accept the treatment plan but never schedule the surgery. Some get stuck waiting on insurance pre-auth.

For a typical OMS practice doing 1,500 referrals/year at an average case value of $4,000, **that's $1.5M to $2.4M of revenue slipping through the front desk** — referrals you already paid for through your relationships with referring dentists.

Your front desk does heroic work. They can't make 50 follow-up calls a day on top of everything else.

---

## What Adapix does

Adapix is a small AI-powered device that lives on your counter. **It follows up on every referral and every consult patient — automatically — until they're scheduled, started, or have said no.**

For each patient it:
- Texts and emails at the right moments — day 1, 3, 7, 14, 21, 30
- Walks them from referral → consult → treatment plan acceptance → scheduled surgery → post-op
- Surfaces financing options proactively for high-ticket cases
- Chases insurance pre-auth without your staff having to babysit it
- Hands clinical questions to **you**, never answers them itself
- Stops the moment a patient says no

Every message is reviewed by your office manager before going out (or fully autonomous once you trust it). The patient never knows it's automated.

---

## How it works (3 steps, 1 hour to set up)

1. **Install:** We bring the device, plug it in, connect to your WiFi. One afternoon.
2. **Review:** Each morning, your office manager reviews the day's outgoing messages on her phone. Approve or edit. Hit send.
3. **Recover:** Adapix delivers, handles replies, books surgeries into your calendar. Patients you would have lost get treated.
