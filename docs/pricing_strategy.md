# Adapix Pricing Strategy

*Locked: November 7, 2025 (decision made between Rocco + co-founder).*

---

## Core principle

Pricing isn't a number — it's a **percentage of value created**. As Adapix moves up-market, both the absolute dollars and the customer's value-recovered grow. The percentage tightens as a volume discount.

| Customer | Value recovered/yr | Adapix charges/yr | % of value |
|---|---|---|---|
| SMB single-location | $200K | $30K | 15% |
| Mid-market | $2M | $60K | 3% |
| Enterprise | $20M | $300K | 1.5% |

The first 50 customers are paying for us to learn pricing — they are NOT the long-term price.

---

## Floor: $2,500/mo

We do not go below $2,500/mo. Period. Anyone smaller than the floor is too small to be worth the support cost.

Anything below $2,500/mo that we accept "to land the deal" trains us to keep doing that and erodes pricing forever.

---

## v1 SMB pricing (locked)

For independent oral & maxillofacial surgery practices in the US, first 100 customers:

| Tier | Profile | Setup | Monthly |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Pilot** | Anyone, 30-day risk-free trial | $5,000 | $2,500 |
| **Solo** | 1 doctor, ≤500 referrals/yr | $7,500 | $2,500 |
| **Group** | 2-3 doctors, ≤1,500 referrals/yr | $10,000 | $3,500 |
| **Multi-location** | 3+ docs OR multi-site | $15,000+ | $5,500+ |

**Guarantee:** 60-day money-back on setup if we haven't recovered a new case start that traces to Adapix. Month-to-month after that. No long-term contracts.

**Discounting policy:** 0% off list. We do not negotiate price for first 50 customers. We are willing to walk away from a deal rather than discount.

---

## Pricing roadmap as Adapix scales

### Phase 1 — Customers 1-50 (year 0-1)
- SMB tier above
- Start with $2,500/mo floor
- Goal: prove product-market fit + dollar outcomes

### Phase 2 — Customers 50-250 (year 1-2)
- Raise SMB floor to $3,500/mo for new customers (existing keep their rate)
- Add **Mid-market tier** for multi-location chains and DSOs:
  - $25,000-50,000 setup
  - $10,000-15,000/mo
  - Annual contracts only
- Continue OMS focus, start expanding to adjacent verticals (general dentistry, ortho, derm, vet)

### Phase 3 — Customers 250-1,000 (year 2-3)
- SMB floor: $5,000/mo
- Mid-market: $15,000-25,000/mo
- Add **Enterprise tier**:
  - $100,000+ setup
  - $25,000-50,000/mo
  - 2-3 year contracts
  - Dedicated CSM + SLA
- Cross-vertical expansion accelerates

### Phase 4 — Customers 1,000+ (year 3+)
- Enterprise becomes the largest revenue contributor
- SMB pricing has risen to $7,500/mo floor
- Hardware refresh cycle starts kicking in (additional revenue from device upgrades every 3-4 years)
- Average customer pays ~$80K/yr fully blended

## The "apartment rent" insight

Rocco's framing: SMB pricing = apartment rent (~$2-3.5K/mo). This is correct AS THE FLOOR.

But it's not the ceiling:

```
SMB:           apartment rent           ($30K/yr)
Mid-market:    small office space rent  ($120K/yr)
Enterprise:    floor of a building      ($600K/yr)
```

Same product. Same hardware. Different value-capture per customer based on their scale.

---

## Pricing is always quoted as ANNUAL once we're talking to mid-market or enterprise

Procurement teams think in annual ACV, not monthly. From customer 100 onward, our sales motion shifts from "$2,500/mo" to "$30,000/year" — same number, different anchor, much easier to budget through procurement.

---

## What this means for v1 messaging

- Public website: pricing only goes up to "Multi-location: contact us" — never publish enterprise prices
- Public website footer says "Enterprise: starting at $50K/year" to set the floor
- Sales calls: always frame in terms of recovered revenue ratio, not monthly cost
- Pilot offer: ALWAYS the 30-day refund on setup, never below $2,500/mo
