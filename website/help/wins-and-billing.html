Wins, your plan & billing

~2 minutes

Track what Adapix wins back

When a chased customer turns into a job, press Won ✓ on their row in the Contacts tab. Adapix asks roughly what the job was worth (pre-filled with the deal value if you imported one) and your Home screen shows the running total: Won back this month: $4,200.

Why bother? That number is how you know the $99 is earning its keep. One recovered job usually pays for months of Adapix — and now you can see it instead of guessing.

The plan

One plan, everything included: $99/month after a 14-day free trial. You add a card to start the trial, but you're charged $0 today — and nothing at all if you cancel before the trial ends. Texts, emails, AI calls, unlimited contacts, and you approve every message.

  • The trial starts at checkout. Adapix doesn't draft or send anything until your card is on file — that's what activates your 14 free days.
  • Founding customers: the first 10 businesses use code FOUNDING at checkout for $49/month, locked for a year.
  • Cancel anytime from Settings → Account & billing. You keep access through the end of the period you already paid for.
  • If a payment fails, Adapix pauses new follow-ups (nothing is deleted) until the card is fixed — you'll see it flagged in Settings → Account & billing.

Referrals — give a month, get a month

Know another owner drowning in quiet quotes? The Referrals page in your sidebar has your personal share link. When a business you refer becomes a paying customer, you get a free month (credited to your bill automatically) and they get an extra free month on top of the normal 14-day trial. The page shows everyone you've referred and exactly where each reward stands.
