FAQ

Questions we hear most.

Does this work with Outlook? Or only Gmail?+
Both. Adapix connects to Google Workspace (Gmail) and Microsoft 365 (Outlook) via OAuth. You sign in once and messages go out from your real practice address — no forwarding or aliases.
Do I need my own Anthropic or Claude account?+
No. Adapix manages the Claude API on your behalf — it's included in your $29/month base plan. You don't need to sign up for anything with Anthropic. The only account you need is Adapix.
What if the device loses power or my WiFi drops?+
Adapt 1.0 includes a UPS battery backup so brief power blips don't interrupt anything. If your internet goes down, outbound messages queue locally and send the moment connectivity restores — nothing is lost. Your dashboard stays accessible on your local network even without internet.
Is this HIPAA compliant? What about sensitive customer data?+
All customer data is AES-256 encrypted and stored only on the Adapt device in your office — it never goes to a third-party cloud. For healthcare, legal, and other regulated industries, we provide a Business Associate Agreement (BAA) as part of every subscription. The AI call is made without sending full records — only the message context needed to compose a reply.
What if I have multiple staff members?+
Unlimited staff logins are included — your whole team sees the same approval queue and inbox. Everyone can review and approve drafts. Multi-location support is available; reach out for a quote.
What if a message goes out wrong — am I liable?+
Adapix requires a human approval tap before any message is sent (unless you switch to auto-mode, which is off by default). Your staff reviews every draft. If something looks off, you edit or reject it. The tool helps you follow up faster — your team stays in control of what actually goes out.
Can I use my existing CRM or business software?+
Yes, via CSV export today. Export your customer or contact list from your CRM, PMS, or spreadsheet and Adapix ingests it in seconds. Direct integrations with popular CRMs and industry-specific software are on the roadmap for Q4 2026. Tell us what software you use and we'll prioritize accordingly.
How does Adapix know what to write? Does it make things up?+
You train Adapix on your business during onboarding — your tone, your services, your follow-up rules. It drafts based only on what you tell it and the customer context you provide. It does not improvise. If a draft looks wrong, you reject it before it goes anywhere.
Can customers opt out of messages?+
Yes. Every outbound message includes a standard opt-out. Adapix removes opted-out contacts from future sequences automatically. You can also manually suppress any contact at any time from the dashboard.
What happens after the pilot ends? Am I locked in?+
No contracts, no lock-in. After the pilot, you move to standard pricing ($799 device + $29/mo base) or you return the hardware under the 30-day return policy. We do not auto-charge without your confirmation.
What exactly does reserving do? Is there a payment now?+
Reserving holds your spot in the first batch — no payment today. Within 48 hours we'll email you to confirm details and discuss whether the pilot program is a fit. If you're selected as a pilot partner, your hardware is free for 60 days. General availability ships Q3 2026.
