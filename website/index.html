FAQ

Questions we hear most.

Does this work with Outlook? Or only Gmail?+
Both. Adapix connects to Google Workspace (Gmail) and Microsoft 365 (Outlook) via secure OAuth. You sign in once and messages go out from your real business address — no forwarding or aliases.
Do I need my own Anthropic or Claude account?+
No. Adapix manages the AI on your behalf — it's included in your plan. You don't need to sign up for anything with Anthropic. The only account you need is Adapix.
Is this software or hardware? What do I need to install?+
Adapix is cloud software — there's nothing to install and no hardware to buy. Sign up, open it in your browser, connect your email, and you're running. It works on any computer, tablet, or phone.
Is this HIPAA compliant? What about sensitive customer data?+
All customer data is AES-256 encrypted in transit and at rest, and isolated per business. For healthcare, legal, and other regulated industries, we provide a Business Associate Agreement (BAA). The AI composes replies from the minimum context needed — it doesn't send full records anywhere.
What if I have multiple staff members?+
Staff logins are included on Growth and Scale plans — your whole team sees the same approval queue and inbox. Everyone can review and approve drafts. Multi-location support is available; reach out for a quote.
What if a message goes out wrong — am I liable?+
Adapix requires a human approval tap before any message is sent (unless you switch on auto-mode, which is off by default). Your staff reviews every draft. If something looks off, you edit or reject it. The tool helps you follow up faster — your team stays in control of what actually goes out.
Can I use my existing CRM or business software?+
Yes, via CSV import today. Export your customer or contact list from your CRM, PMS, or spreadsheet and Adapix ingests it in seconds. Direct integrations with popular CRMs are on the roadmap. Tell us what software you use and we'll prioritize accordingly.
How does Adapix know what to write? Does it make things up?+
You train Adapix on your business during onboarding — your tone, your services, your follow-up rules. It drafts based only on what you tell it and the customer context you provide. It does not improvise. If a draft looks wrong, you reject it before it goes anywhere.
Can customers opt out of messages?+
Yes. Every outbound message includes a standard opt-out. Adapix removes opted-out contacts from future sequences automatically. You can also manually suppress any contact at any time from the dashboard.
Am I locked into a contract?+
No contracts, no lock-in. Start with a 14-day free trial, then pick a monthly plan. Cancel anytime from your account — we never auto-charge without your confirmation at the end of the trial.
What does the free trial include? Is there a payment now?+
The 14-day free trial gives you full access with no credit card and no payment today. Connect your email, import contacts, and let Adapix start drafting follow-ups. If it's a fit, pick a plan when the trial ends — otherwise it simply stops, no charge.
